SatScanner 1.6-r0

- Live Feed integration: when opened from the Live Feed plugin, scan results are added to the Live Feed bouquet and the selected service plays from there.
- Multiple services found are all added to the Live Feed bouquet; the one you pick is played.
- Keeps the "SatScanner" title (and skips the Last Scanned bouquet) when launched by Live Feed.

SatScanner 1.5-r0

- Update check now runs on every plugin open (re-prompts if you dismissed it).
- Fixed a crash on the "update installed" screen (modal open race).
- Update installer shows live progress and defers the Enigma2 restart to the plugin.

SatScanner 1.4.0-r1

- SDT-based transponder scanner (builds correct service references from the SDT).
- Feed-safe: reconciles scanned refs with the box's lamedb namespace/onid.
- Live signal finder (LED bar, SNR, lock) with reliable lock detection.
- Scan Mode: All channels / TV channels only.
- Last Scanned bouquet + add/replace/create/remove bouquet management.
- Available in Service Searching, Plugin Browser, and the Extensions menu.
